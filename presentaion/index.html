UniMeet

Why UniMeet?

  • New student struggles

    Freshman during Covid-19 have very limited opportunities to meet new people outside of there limited friends

  • Combating Loneliness

    Many students are in almost entirely asynchronous classes and can develop a feeling of loneliness due to limited social interactions

  • Building an online community

    For some students, meeting people in person is not an option due to the real risk Covid-19 poses to them

Overview

  • Platform to connect college students
  • Pairs students based on interests
  • Sign up using school email
  • Select meeting time(s) to be matched
  • View and like other profiles to further pair effectively
